Interior and Exterior Services That Go the Distance

Our detailing packages cover everything from deep carpet extraction and leather conditioning to paint decontamination and ceramic protection. Whether it’s road grime, pet hair, or hard-to-reach buildup, we’ve got it covered.

For the exterior, we focus on:

  • Thorough wash and decontamination
  • Paint claying to remove embedded contaminants
  • Machine polishing to reduce swirl marks and restore gloss
  • Paint sealant or ceramic coatings for extended protection

Inside, we take care of:

  • Vacuuming and shampooing all surfaces
  • Leather conditioning and UV protection
  • Cleaning vents, cup holders, and console areas
  • Odor neutralizing and bacteria removal

Our full-service car detailing isn’t just for one-time events. It’s an investment in how your vehicle performs, looks, and feels every day.

Ceramic Coating for Long-Lasting Paint Protection

Want to go a step further in protecting your vehicle’s exterior? Our ceramic coating service offers an extra layer of defense against the elements. It’s ideal for Ohio’s harsh weather and road conditions.

Ceramic coatings provide a hydrophobic surface that repels water, dirt, and UV rays. The result? Your car stays cleaner longer, and the paintwork maintains its depth and clarity for years. It’s a favorite among car enthusiasts and practical drivers alike.

And don’t worry, we’ll walk you through what’s involved and help you decide whether this is the right option for your car and your driving lifestyle.
